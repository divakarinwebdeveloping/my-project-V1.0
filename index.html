Access major attractions from our prime location

Kazakhstan's young capital is a treat for personal and business travelers alike. Enjoy its exciting culture and stylish buzz in our contemporary Park Inn by Radisson in Nur-Sultan. Boasting a prime location in the business district and next to the Arman complex corporate center, our hotel in Nur-Sultan is the ultimate base for both access and exploration. Enjoy Nur-Sultan's dynamic culture when you have a moment or two to yourself or want to entertain guests. To take in the best of this beautiful city, visit the iconic opera and ballet theater, Astana Opera, which puts on modern hits and all of the classics. The M.Gorky Russian Drama Theatre stages an array of lively shows in a 19th-century building. For a hint of political history, find the Museum of the First President just 1.5 kilometers away from our Nur-Sultan hotel.
